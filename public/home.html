Welcome to unWrapped — a party planning application

Let's face it, party planning takes a lot of time, and throwing an excellent party takes more time than most of us have. From keeping track of the guest list to remembering who gave you what gift, it is enough to make anyone want to pull their hair out. That is where unWrapped comes in.

We help to make things simple.

All you will need to do is create the party and supply your guests with the party code. They'll use unWrapped to RSVP to your party. Not only will we keep track of the guest list, we also help remembering who gave you what gift and if you sent a thank-you note.

So, if you're planning your next party, go ahead and sign up. We won't alleviate all of your stress — you still have to order the cake — but we will certainly make it easier so you can enjoy making memories to last a life time.
